THE REAL METRIC (IMPORTANT)

Each page must achieve:

1. Intent Coverage Score (ICS)
   → Did you fully answer what the searcher wants?

2. Authority Depth
   → Do you sound like a specialist or a content writer?

3. Conversion Readiness
   → Is the user ready to take action after reading?
